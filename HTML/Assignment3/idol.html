Education

Lee majored in Professional Motion Pictures and Art at Konkuk University and gratuated in 2016.

Career

At 15, he debuted as a runway model at the Seoul Collection walkway in 2005. Lee trained as an idol group member for three months. In 2010, he official actiong debut in the South Korean TV series Prosecutor Princess is made. Lee's breakthrough role was in 2013(2012)as a high school student. Also, he is well known for his role in I Can Hear Your Voice(2013), Doctor Stranger(2014),Pinocchio(2014),W(2016),While You Were Sleeping(2017),Romance is a Bonus Book(2019).

Hobbies

Lee's hobbies are playing basketball and Chess. While his skills are Taekwando and the piano.
